IOT4.US

A simpler way to make your own home a smart home.

Who says you need to spend
a lot of money and time
to make your home yours?

No need to learn how to code!

We created a simple drag and drop interface to allow for simple, yet powerful programs to control your home. Customize it all to your liking right on your tablet, whether you're home, or on the go!

Integrate with any sensor!

The system works with a very flexible OS, which allows it to work with anything as low level as a temperature sensor to anything as high as a webcam!

Time is money!

We know technology can be expensive and annoying! We remove the expensive cost of development and support so that you can make your home your own!

IFTT Integration

We know some of you already have existing workflows. No worries! IOT4.US integrates easily into your IFTT workflows.
